Reports switch statements/expressions with too low a ratio of switch labels to executable statements. Such switch statements may be confusing, and should probably be refactored.

Example of the reported code:

    switch (i) { // one case and 5 executable statements -> 20% density
        case 1:
            System.out.println("1");
            System.out.println("2");
            System.out.println("3");
            System.out.println("4");
            System.out.println("5");
            break;
    }

Use the field below to specify allowed ration of the switch labels to executable statements.
