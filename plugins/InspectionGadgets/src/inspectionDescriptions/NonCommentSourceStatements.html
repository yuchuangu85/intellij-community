Reports methods that have too many statements. Methods that have too many statements may be confusing, and are a good sign that refactoring is necessary.

Note: all statements except the following are counted:

  • empty statements (semicolon)
  • block statements
  • for loop initialization statement i.e. in for(int i = ...;...) statement int i = ... is not counted
  • for loop update statement i.e. in for(int i = ...;...; i += 2) statement i += 2 is not counted

Use the field below to specify the maximum number of statements a method is allowed to have.
